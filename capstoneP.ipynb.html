Introduction¶

"This dataset contains current job postings available on the City of New Yorks official jobs site (http://www.nyc.gov/html/careers/html/search/search.shtml). Internal postings available to city employees and external postings available to the general public are included.the dataset is hosted by the City of New York. The city has an open data platform found(https://opendata.cityofnewyork.us/) and they update their information according the amount of data that is brought in. Explore New York City using Kaggle and all of the data sources available through the City of New York organization page!

Update Frequency: This dataset is updated weekly."
